Are you ready to build MINI ROBO with ADDS!!!

Here is the list of components you will be needing.

  1. Tall identical jars or bottles, such as empty, clear, plastic 1-liter or 2-liter bottles (2).
  2. Timer or clock that shows seconds.
  3. Water.
  4. Food coloring.
  5. Vegetable oil (enough to fill the jars nearly full).
  6. An Alka-Seltzer tablet. Only one tablet is needed for the activity.
  7. Cutting board
  8. Knife

simply follow are easy steps to build your own project.so here we goo!!

  • step 1: To each jar or bottle, fill it with 1-2 inches of water, add 5 drops of food coloring, and then fill it at least three-quarters full with vegetable oil. Put the cap on tightly to avoid spills and leaks.

  • step 2: Once one jar is hot and one is cold, get a timer or clock ready and drop a quarter of a tablet into the heated jar. Note that the tablet piece may take a moment to sink through the vegetable oil to reach the water, where it will react. Start timing as soon as the tablet piece reaches the water and starts reacting.

  • step 3: Now drop a quarter of a tablet in the cold jar. Time how long it takes the tablet to disappear this time.

  • step 4:Cleanup
    When you are done playing with the lava lamps, do not pour them down the drain. That quantity of vegetable oil can lead to clogs in the sewer system. Instead, pour the contents of the lava lamps into a compost pile or dig a hole in your yard, away from any plants you care about, and pour the contents in the hole. If neither of those are an option, simply screw the cap on tightly and throw the lava lamp jar in your regular trash.

And thats it, here you are ready with your project.
