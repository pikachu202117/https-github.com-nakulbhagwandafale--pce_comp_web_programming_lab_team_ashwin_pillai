Are you ready to build Violin Generator with ADDS!!!

Here is the list of components you will be needing.

  1. 9 volt toy battery
  2. Wooden block
  3. Bicycle Spoke Bow
  4. Two LED's
  5. Rubber glue
  6. Pulley
  7. Bottle caps
  8. Wires

simply follow are easy steps to build your own project. so here we goo!!

  • step 1: FIxed a Pulley on motor shaft.

  • step 2:Make holes in two bottle caps and mount one LED in each.

  • step 3: Attach the two LED's to the mtor terminal as shown.

  • step 4: Fix the motor and LED's on a wooden base. On flicking the pulley clockwise, the left LED will light. on flicking the pully anti-clockwise, the right LED will light.

  • step 5:use a string bow to spin the pully continuously. Move bow back and forth to See the LED's glow alternately.

And thats it, here you are ready with your project.
