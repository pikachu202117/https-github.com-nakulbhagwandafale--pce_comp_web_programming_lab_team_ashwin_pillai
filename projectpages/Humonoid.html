Are you ready to build A HUMANOID ROBOT with ADDS!!!

Here is the list of components you will be needing.

  1. Control 18 hobby servos from PC and Microcontroller
  2. USB interface
  3. Comes Pre-loaded Arduino Uno bootloader
  4. Software exports servo sequences to Arduino Uno for running servo sequences
  5. Independent range setting for each servo
  6. Independent offset, Maximum, Minimum and Direction setting for each servo
  7. 0.5-microsecond resolution
  8. 50 Hz update rate
  9. Small size of 80 X 47 mm
  10. Plug and Play, Auto detection of hardware
  11. Servo sequencer with speed, delay, goto and many other features
  12. Bluetooth interface for wireless control of robots
  13. M4 x 12 Screw - M4 Screw
  14. M4 x 12 Screw Nut - M4 Nut
  15. 3 x 6 Servo Screw - Servo Screw
  16. Miniature Ball Radial Bearing - Bearing
  17. 3 mm Nylock Nut - Lock nut,3 mm Nut - Nut
  18. 3 x 6 Button Head Screw - Small Button Screw
  19. 3 x 10 Button Head Screw - Big Button Screw
  20. 3 x 6 CSK Screw - CSK Screw
  21. M3 x 8 Screw - M3 Mid Screw
  22. Metal Horn for Servo 25T - Servo Horn
  23. Metal Gear Standard Servo (or) Servo Economy - Servo Motor
  24. . Multipurpose Aluminium Standard Servo Bracket - Multipurpose Bracket
  25. Short U Shape Aluminium Servo Bracket - Short U Bracket
  26. Long U Aluminium Servo Bracket - Long U Bracket
  27. Oblique U Shape Aluminium Servo Bracket - Oblique U Bracket
  28. Interconnect Aluminium Servo Bracket - Interconnect Bracket
  29. L Shaped Interconnect Servo Bracket - L Shaped Bracket
  30. Large U Beam Aluminium Servo Bracket - Large U Bracket
  31. Robot feet Aluminium Servo Bracket - Robot Feet
