Are you ready to Make a CIRCUIT GREETING CARD with ADDS!!!

Here is the list of components you will be needing.

  1. Light emitting diode of any color
  2. Copper tape with adhesive of 5mm
  3. A clear tape
  4. Black colored A4 sheet paper
  5. A4 size paper
  6. Glue
  7. color paper of your choice for making cupcake
  8. Button or coin cell of 3 Volt
  9. Scissor

simply follow are easy steps to build your own project.so here we goo!!

  • step 1: Design a basic circuit:

    Draw a layout of paper circuit card as shown in the image.
    Take a copper tape having adhesive on one side and trace on the circuit diagram.
    Make sure to continue the tape at corners.
    For the corners, bend the tape at 45 degree opposite to the direction we are going and then trace on the line. This way continuity of circuit is assured.
    Bend the legs of LED at 90 degree and secure with a tape. The positive of LED should be connected to positive of cell and negative of LED to negative of cell leaving a gap for switch.
    The switch which a small strip of copper tape is at a distance few cms away from gap.
    By folding the paper the circuit gets complete and the LED lights up. In the next steps I present a few paper circuit card made by me.

  • step 2: Take a black A4 size paper and fold in half.

  • step 3: Follow the Images to make cupcake and a candle.

  • step 4: Insert the LED from flame of candle and bend the legs of LED at 90 degree inside the card.

  • step 5: Follow step 1 of instructable to know how to make a basic circuit.

And thats it, here you are ready with your project.
