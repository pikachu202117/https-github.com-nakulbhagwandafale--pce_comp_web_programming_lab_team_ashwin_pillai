Are you ready to Make a SLIM MAGNET with ADDS!!!

Here is the list of components you will be needing.

  1. liquid starc
  2. Glue
  3. iron oxide powder
  4. magnets
  5. craft sticks
  6. tablespoon
  7. disposable cups

simply follow are easy steps to build your own project.so here we goo!!

  • step 1:Add a tablespoon of iron oxide powder into your disposable cup or bowl.

  • step 2: Add 2 tablespoons of liquid starch. Use a craft stick to mix the ingredients until the liquid is uniform and smooth.

  • step 3: Add 2 tablespoons of white glue and stir your mixture until you get an even consistency. You may need to scrape the mixture off your craft stick periodically to keep mixing.

  • step 4: At this point, we put on our gloves as things start to get a little messy!

    Scoop out the black slime and knead it with your hands. It should start to dry as you do so. Keep kneading until your slime is smooth and dry. If needed, you can gently dab it on a paper towel to help with the drying process.
