Are you ready to Make a SNOW BLOWERwith ADDS!!!

Here is the list of components you will be needing.

  1. 3v dc motor
  2. Copper tape with adhesive of 5mmaa batteries (2)
  3. battery pack
  4. hobby propeller fan
  5. plastic cups (2) (large, clear)
  6. paper (white, blue)
  7. color paper of your choice for making cupcake
  8. Tape
  9. Scissor
  10. push pin
  11. pencil

simply follow are easy steps to build your own project.so here we goo!!

  • step 1: Put the batteries in the connector pack. Hook the leads of the battery pack into the holes on the bottom of the motor. Twist the leads so they stay in place..

  • step 2: Use the pushpin to poke a hole in the bottom of one cup (aim for the middle!). Poke the hole with a pencil to widen the hole.

  • step 3: Use tape to attach the motor to the bottom exterior of the cup. Make sure the shaft of the motor is aligned to poke into the cup. Tape the battery pack to the side of the cup.

  • step 4:Lower the propeller fan into the cup so that it fits onto the motor shaft. Turn on the battery pack to give your motor and fan a test run. The fan should start spinning!

  • step 5:Time to make snow! Cut your paper into small bits of confetti-like squares (ours were about quarter-inch squares).

  • step 6: Pour your "snow" into your empty cup. Now, tape the two cups together, mouth-to-mouth. Use short strips of single-sided tape to hold them in place, then wrap around with a long piece to cover the seam.

And thats it, here you are ready with your project.
