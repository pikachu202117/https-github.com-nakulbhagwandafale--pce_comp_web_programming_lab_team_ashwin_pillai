Are you ready to build Lifting Jack with ADDS!!!

Here is the list of components you will be needing.

  1. Ice cream sticks
  2. Copper wire
  3. Cardboard Pieces
  4. Needle
  5. Two syringes (5 ml)
  6. Flexi-tube

simply follow are easy steps to build your own project. so here we goo!!

  • step 1:Make three holes in eah stick with a needle.

  • step 2:Bend copper wire ends Into loops as stoppers. Join two sticks with coppe wire, as shown.

  • step 3: Make lazy tongs with ice cream sticks.

  • step 4: Join two lazy tongs together at the base with cross sticks.

  • step 5: Make slits on two sticks and attach them on top of the lazy tongs.

  • step 6: Stick a cardboard platform on top.

  • step 7: Fix the assembly on a cardboard base. One end of the tongs should be free and other fixed..

  • step 8: Insert the upper end of the syringe Into one end of the flex-tube.

  • step 9:Fill the syring with water.

  • step 10: Fix another syringe to the other end of the flex-tube.

  • step 11: Tie one syringe to the cardboard base and tie Its plunger to the free end of the lazy tongs.

  • step 12: On pushing the other plunger, the lazy tongs will lift the platform. Repeat the experiment to lift sorne weight.

And thats it, here you are ready with your project.
