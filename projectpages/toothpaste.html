Are you ready to build Toothpaste Pump with ADDS!!!

Here is the list of components you will be needing.

  1. Empty toothpaste tube
  2. Scissors
  3. Ballon
  4. Water mug
  5. Stiff straw (5cm)

simply follow are easy steps to build your own project. so here we goo!!

  • step 1: Cut an Empyt toothpaste tube 4cm from the open end. Wash it well to remove any sticking toothpaste.

  • step 2:Fix the ballon on the threaded mouth of the tube.

  • step 3: Cut the ballon as shown the cut ballon will act like a valve.

  • step 4: Make a hole near the closed end of the tube insert a stiff straw in the hole as the delivery pipe.

  • step 5:Insert the ballon valve in the open tube this will complete the pump.

  • step 6:Hold the Pump vertically and move it up-down in a mug of water. After a while, water will squirt out from the delivery pipe.

And thats it, here you are ready with your project.
