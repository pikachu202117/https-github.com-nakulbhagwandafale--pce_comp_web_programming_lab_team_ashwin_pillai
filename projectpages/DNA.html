Are you ready to build an DNA Helix with ADDS!!!

Here is the list of components you will be needing.

  1. Scissors
  2. Two penciles
  3. Plastic straws ( four color)
  4. color cello tapes

simply follow are easy steps to build your own project.so here we goo!!

  • step 1: Cut 6 cm long pleces from the coloured straws.

  • step 2: Tape straws of two different colours .

  • step 3: Stick the coloured straws alternately between two parallel sticky tapes. Stick pencils on both ends to hold the model.

  • step 4: Stick another layer of tape on top. The straws will be sandwiched between two layers of tape..

  • step 5: Hold the model on both ends and twist it to see a beautiful DNA HELIX. .

And thats it, here you are ready with your project.
