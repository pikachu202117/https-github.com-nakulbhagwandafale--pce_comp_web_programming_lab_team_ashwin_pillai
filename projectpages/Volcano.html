Are you ready to Make a VOLCANO with ADDS!!!

Here is the list of components you will be needing.

  1. 6 cups of Flour
  2. 2 cups of Water
  3. 2 cups of Salt
  4. Coloring
  5. Red Food Coloring
  6. A few drops of Dish Soap- about a Tablespoo
  7. 2 Tbsp Baking Soda
  8. Vinegar

simply follow are easy steps to build your own project.so here we goo!!

  • step 1: Mix the best you can with a spoon, but you may need to just dig in with your hands. That's what I had to do. This is a hard dough so you can mold it and make it stand up around the bottle for your volcano filling. You may need to add a little bit more water depending on your climate. I added 1/4-1/3 cup more after I got into it.

  • step 2:You will need a bottle in the center. You can use a soda bottle a water bottle or a glass bottle like we used. Whatever you have on hand that has a smaller opening on top should work just fine!

  • step 3:Place the bottle in the center of a large cookie sheet. Mold the salt dough into a volcano shape around the bottle making sure to leave an opening at the top to add your volcano's “lava” filling

  • step 4:Mix the red coloring, water, and some dish soap together. We used about 2 cups of water, but you'll just need to fill your bottle about 3/4 of the way full.

  • step 5:Put 2 Tbsp of baking soda into the bottle.

  • step 6:Pour in your vinegar and watch the eruption go! Now is the perfect time to teach about the chemical reaction between the baking soda and vinegar.

And thats it, here you are ready with your project.
