Are you ready to build Stick Transformation with ADDS!!!

Here is the list of components you will be needing.

  1. Scissors
  2. Two penciles
  3. Plastic straws ( four color)
  4. color cello tapes

simply follow are easy steps to build your own project.so here we goo!!

  • step 1: Arrange matchsticks as shown.

  • step 2: Place B across A.

  • step 3: Place C across B .

  • step 4:Place D across C and A.

  • step 5: Now, repeatedly press and release matchstick D.

  • step 6: Increase the length of the chain by placing more sticks in a similar manner. On pressing the last matchstick, the first matchstick will lift.

And thats it, here you are ready with your project.
