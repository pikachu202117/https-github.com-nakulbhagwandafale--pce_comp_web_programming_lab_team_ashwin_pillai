Are you ready to build an electric with ADDS!!!

Here is the list of components you will be needing.

  • 1.5 volt battery
  • strong mangnet
  • card doll
  • rubber base
  • two pencils
  • bare copper rod 5cm
  • wooden stick
  • copper wire

simply follow are easy steps to build your own project.so here we goo!!

  • step 1: join tow 40cm long capper wires to the two ends of a 5cm long bare copper rod.

  • step 2: hang to wires on inverted U-shaped swing made from two pencil and a stick.

  • step 3: glue the doll to the copper rod seat.

  • step 4: stick a strong mangnet on one end and place a battery on the other end, on the rubber base, as shown

  • step 5: stand the swings in the two holes of yhe rubber base. on connecting and disconnecting the wire to the battery terminals, the doll will start swinging back and forth.

And thats it, here you are ready with your project.
