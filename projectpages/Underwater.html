Are you ready to Make a UNDERWATER FIREWORK with ADDS!!!

Here is the list of components you will be needing.

  1. bottle
  2. warm water
  3. salt
  4. cool water
  5. oil
  6. food coloring
  7. stirring stick
  8. Small Bowl

simply follow are easy steps to build your own project.so here we goo!!

  • step 1: Pour salt into the bottle until there is a thin layer of salt on the bottom.

  • step 2: Pour warm water into the bottle so that the bottle is about ¼ full. Mix the salt and water until all of the salt has dissolved and the water has cooled.

  • step 3: Pour cold freshwater into the bottle until the water level is about ¾ high.

  • step 4: Pour oil into the bottle, so that there is about ½” of oil floating on top of the water.

  • step 5: Add a spoonful of oil into a small bowl followed by a few drops of food dye.

  • step 6: Stir until the large beads of dye have broken up into a bunch of small beads of dye.

  • step 7: Gently pour the mixture into the bottle. Then, watch as the food dye begins to seep into the water! What happens when it reaches the saltwater layer?

And thats it, here you are ready with your project.
