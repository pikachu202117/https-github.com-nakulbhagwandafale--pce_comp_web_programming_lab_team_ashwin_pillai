Are you ready to Make a GLOWING GELATIN with ADDS!!!

Here is the list of components you will be needing.

  1. gelatin (one packet) (lime or yellow work best)
  2. saucepan
  3. tonic water (2 cups)
  4. spoon
  5. liquid measuring cup
  6. A few drops of Dish Soap- about a Tablespoo
  7. glass cup or jar
  8. blacklight

simply follow are easy steps to build your own project.so here we goo!!

  • step 1:Heat up 1 cup of tonic water over the stove until it begins to boil.

  • step 2: Pour the flavored gelatin packet into the glass container. Carefully add in the boiling tonic water and stir until all the gelatin is fully dissolved.

  • step 3:To help cool the mixture, slowly add a second cup of (non-boiling) tonic water. Be careful when pouring since the solution might fizz.

And thats it, here you are ready with your project.Tonic water will make your gelatin glow more brightly under a black light, however, it will have a slightly bitter taste. You can replace this cup of tonic water with normal water if you prefer.
